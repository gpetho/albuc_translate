E aprop pausa en quascuna fissura calgua de coto vielh, e pausa sobre aquels polvils enbegutz en vi e oli e vi agre, e vegas que no endevengua apostema caut.
Then place old cotton in each fissure, and place powders soaked in wine and oil and sour wine on them, and ensure that no hot abscess occurs.

E en le tertz dia deslia le liament e aministra gran enfusio de aygua cauda o tebeza, e aprop cura aquo am enguent bazilicon am oli rozat e autres entro que las plaguas sian sanadas.
And on the third day, untie the bandage and administer a large infusion of warm or tepid water, and then treat it with basilicon ointment with rose oil and others until the wounds are healed.

Chapter 5.

De la cura de lagramas per catartz e als huels descendentz de las partidas dedintz del cap.
On the treatment of tears from catarrh and those descending to the eyes from the inner parts of the head.

Quan ad alcu veno catartz agutz e continuantz, e vezes les huels del malaute extenuatz petitz, e ja le reguardament de lor es debelitat, e las palpebras ulceradas e de lor cazo les pels, e en le preon del cap es dolor aguda ledent e extrenutacio consequent, lahoras sapias per aquestz accidentz que las materias e catartz no veno sino de motas venas de las partidas dedintz procedent.
When someone has acute and continuous catarrh, and sometimes the patient's eyes are strained, and their vision is weakened, and the eyelids ulcerated and losing their eyelashes, and there is sharp pain in the head and consequent sneezing, then know from these symptoms that the matter and catarrh come from many veins of the internal parts.

E la milhor curacio en aquels es aquesta: que comandes al malaute que raza lo sieu front, e aprop inscideys en aquel una fissura al mieg loc del front, un petit per la latitut (e comensa am la fissura del templar senestre entro al dextre templar; e sia inscisio entro al os; e eslongua la tua ma dels lacertz del templas lesquals son maugutz deves la mastigacio), entro que sia le os descubert.
And the best treatment for them is this: instruct the patient to shave their forehead, and then make an incision in the middle of the forehead, slightly across the width (and start with the incision from the left temple to the right temple; and let the incision be to the bone; and extend your hand away from the nerves of the temples which are damaged towards the chewing), until the bone is exposed.

Exsicca tot le sanc am espongua o am drap secc e aprop repleys entre las doas labias de la fissura am coto vielh o am draps de li.
Dry all the blood with a sponge or dry cloth and then fill between the two lips of the fissure with old cotton or linen cloths.

E aprop estrenh desus am polvils enbegutz en vi e oli per so que no endevengua apostema caut.
Then tighten above with powders soaked in wine and oil so that no hot abscess occurs.

E quan deslias aquo e veses que l’apostema ja es diminuit, lahoras cove que fregua le os entro que comensa en lu nayser carn, e aprop cura aquel am regiment exsiccatiu le qual fassa neysser la carn, ayssi cum si tu prenes de farina de froment dos partidas e de colofania quatre partidas e mescla o, e de lor sia fayt emplastre e sia aministrat en aquestas plaguas a de la carn nativitat.
And when you untie this and see that the abscess has already diminished, then rub the bone until flesh starts to grow, and then treat it with a drying regimen that makes the flesh grow, as if you take two parts wheat flour and four parts colophony and mix it, and make a plaster from it and apply it to these wounds until the flesh grows naturally.

Chapter 6.

De la cura de aquo que catz en las aurelhas.
On the treatment of what falls into the ears.

Tota causa que catz en las aurelhas es una de quatre specias, quar o es peyra mineral o causa semblant a peyra, ayssi cum ferr e veyre, o es de planta ayssi cum ceze o fava, o es causa fluxtibla ayssi cum aygua e vi agre e a lor semblantz, o alcuna bestia.
Everything that falls into the ears is one of four types, for it is either a mineral stone or something similar to a stone, like iron and glass, or it is from a plant like a pea or bean, or it is a flexible thing like water and sour wine and similar substances, or some animal.

E donc quan peyra catz en l’aurelha o autra causa semblant de aquelas causas que no ajustan ne creysso en l’aurelha, e pausa la aurelha al solelh, e si vezes la peyra, lahoras distilla en aquela alcuna causa de oli violat o sizami, e aprop aministra la extraccio de lu, e fay le stornudar am condizi, e oppila las nars deve le aveniment de stornutacio.
And so when a stone falls into the ear or another similar thing that does not get stuck or grow in the ear, hold the ear to the sun, and if you see the stone, then drip some violet or sesame oil into it, and then administer its extraction, and make the person sneeze, and block the nostrils to prevent sneezing.

E aprop pausa en le circuit de la aurelha alscunas pessas de drap o lana, e dextendas la aurelha a las partidas desus, quar motas veguadas es foro-getada am aquestas causas.
Then place some pieces of cloth or wool around the ear, and extend the ear upwards, as many times it is expelled with these actions.

E si no n’es getada, lahoras aministra extraccio de lu am le instrument apelat gesti epicicarios subtil del qual aquesta es la forma:
And if it is not expelled, then administer its extraction with the instrument called "gesti epicicarios," which has this shape:

E si es foro-getada am gesti, bo es; e sino, lahoras aministra la extraccio de lu am onci subtil de pauca duplicacio.
And if it is expelled with "gesti," good; if not, then administer its extraction with a subtle hook of little duplication.

E donc si no es foro-getada per aquo, lahoras fay una canula de aram e entromet la extremitat de la canula en le trauc de la aurelha e claus a l’enviro de la canula am cera e am oli mollificada per so que no sia ubert sino la canula.
And if it is not expelled by this, then make a brass cannula and insert the end of the cannula into the hole of the ear and seal around the cannula with wax and softened oil so that only the cannula is open.

E aprop extratz am le tieu vent am fort atraccio, quar tropas veguadas cum aquo que havem recontat.
Then extract with your breath with strong suction, for many times with this we have succeeded.

Mays si no es foro-getada en aquesta manieyra, lahoras pren de halka alumbat o de vecz aparelhat am le qual son preses les ausels una patita quantitat, e pausa aquel en la extremitat de ray, e aprop envolopa sobre aquel coto decentment pausat.
But if it is not expelled in this way, then take some "alum" or "vecz" used for catching birds in a small amount, and place it on the end of a stick, and then wrap it with cotton decently placed.

E aprop entromet aquel en le trauc de la aurelha am facilitat, e aprop excicca la aurelha de la humiditat.
Then insert it into the hole of the ear easily, and then dry the ear of moisture.

E donc si no es foro-getada am totas las causas las quals havem recontadas, lahoras tu coyta a talhar aquela denant que endevengua apostema caut o espasme.
And if it is not expelled with all the methods we have described, then hurry to cut it before it becomes a hot abscess or spasm.

E la manieyra de talhar aquela es que tu al comensament sancnes le malaute de la vena sephasica e extratz del sanc segon la mannieyra de la vertut de lu, e aprop fay aquel cezer entre las tuas mas e converteys l’aurelha a las partidas desus, e fen am petita fissura en la razit de la aurelha deves lo greys de lu en le loc be premegut de lu.
And the way to cut it is to first bleed the patient from the cephalic vein and extract blood according to its virtue, then make the patient sit between your hands and turn the ear upwards, and make a small incision at the root of the ear towards the thickness of it in the well-pressed place.

E sia la fissura de figura lunar entro que pervengua a la peyra, e, la peyra atrobada, osta aquella si podes am instrumentz apropriatz; e aprop cos la fissura en aquela hora am velocitat, e cura aquela entro que sia sanat.
And let the incision be of a crescent shape until it reaches the stone, and, having found the stone, remove it if you can with appropriate instruments; then sew the incision immediately with speed, and treat it until it is healed.

E si la causa cazent en la aurelha es un dels gras que creysso e so inflatz, lahoras aministra la curacio e la extraccio de lu; mays si no obezeys a tu ad eyssiment, lahoras tu pren spatum teune subtil segon aquesta forma:
And if the thing falling into the ear is one of the fats that grow and swell, then administer the treatment and extraction of it; but if it does not obey you to come out, then take a thin and subtle spatula of this shape:

e aministra am aquel la abscizio de aquel gra lo qual es cazut en l’aurelha.
and use it for the excision of that lump which has fallen into the ear.

E no fassas aquo quan cogitas que aquel ja es humectat per la vapor del aurelha, e pausa aquel en motas pessas, e prop extratz ne aquelas am les oncis o am gesti subtil, o ab inscizio ayssi co dit havem, quar es alleujada la extraccio de lu.
And do not do this when you think it is already moistened by the vapor of the ear, and place it in many pieces, and then extract it with hooks or subtle gestures, or by incision as we have said, for its extraction is made easier.

Mays en l’aygua la qual es intrada en l’aurelha cove que·l malaute use de sternutacio am condizi al comensament, e omple la sieua aurelha am alcu coto vielh, e jagua sobre aquela aurelha en la qual es l’aygua, e si per aquo es foro-getada, bo es; e sino, lahoras pren tropas peyras teunas e leons segon la longitut del dit, e calfa aquelas en le foc un pauc, e le pacient entrometa una de aquelas en le trauc de la sua aurelha e saute sobre la un pe sieu de aquela partida e percussia am una autra peyra sobre la peyra la qual es dintz la aurelha.
But for water that has entered the ear, the patient should sneeze at the beginning, and fill the ear with some old cotton, and lie on the ear with the water in it, and if this expels it, good; if not, then take some thin and long stones according to the length of a finger, and heat them a little in the fire, and the patient should insert one of them into the hole of their ear and jump on one foot of that side and strike with another stone on the stone inside the ear.

Donc no cesse aquo am peyra entro que tota l’aygua sia foro-getada.
Then do not stop with the stone until all the water is expelled.

E ja l’aygua sira foro-getada enayssi que tu prenguas de papier o de gonc .i.,
And the water will already be expelled if you take some paper or cloth,

e met la una extremetat de lu en l’aurelha e estendas l’autre extremitat am foc entro que sia comburit le may de lu.
and place one end of it in the ear and extend the other end over the fire until most of it is burnt.

E prop met i altre gonc e fay aquo tantas veguadas entro que tota l’aygua sia foro-getada, e stratz aquel am canula ayssi co desus es dit de la peyra extrasadora.
And then put another cloth and do this many times until all the water is expelled, and extract it with a cannula as said above for extracting the stone.

Mays en la extraccio de bestia intrant en aquela aurelha, si es a petit cos, ayssi piussa e a lu semblant, cura aquel ayssi cum havem dit en la divisio; mays si es de gran cos la qual causa apar al sen del vezement, lahoras aministra la extraccio de lu am gesti e oncis, e la causa de lu es pus leugiera de totas las autras causas que cazo en l’aurelha.
But for the extraction of an animal entering that ear, if it is a small thing like a flea or something similar, treat it as we have said in the division; but if it is a large thing that is visible to the senses, then administer its extraction with hooks and forceps, and the reason for it is easier than all other things that fall into the ear.

Mays en la extraccio de verm engendrat en la aurelha, quan tu curas aquel am aquo que havem dit en la divisio e en le tractat de distillacios e no comfereys la tua curacio, lahoras cove que tu reguardes la aurelha al solelh.
But for the extraction of a worm generated in the ear, when you treat it with what we have said in the division and in the treatise on distillations and it does not work, then you need to hold the ear to the sun.

E donc si apar aqui alcuna causa dels verms, lahoras extratz aquel am gesti o am onci subtils, mays si no apar deguna causa, lahoras pren una canula dejos streyta e desus ampla de la qual aquesta es la forma de lu:
And if some cause of the worms appears, then extract it with hooks or subtle forceps, but if no cause appears, then take a cannula narrow at the bottom and wide at the top, which has this shape:

E entromet la extremitat subtil en la aurelha segon la quantitat la qual suffertar puesca le malaute; e aprop sucgeys am aquela per succio fort, e fay aquo motas veguadas, entro que totz les verms sian foro-gitatz.
And insert the subtle end into the ear according to the amount the patient can tolerate; then suck with it strongly, and do this many times until all the worms are expelled.

Mays si no obezeysso a tu al ayssiment, lahoras tu opila e claus en le circuit de la canula am cera ayssi cum havem dit; lahoras aministre distillacios las quals han ja esproadas les antics en la curacio dels verms, e atrobaras aquo en le tractat de distillacios.
But if it does not obey you to come out, then seal and close around the cannula with wax as we have said; then administer distillations that the ancients have already tried in the treatment of worms, and you will find this in the treatise on distillations.

E sia la tua efusio am la qual efundas les olis medicinas en la aurelha am aquest strument del qual aquesta es la forma de lu:
And let your infusion with which you pour the medicinal oils into the ear be done with this instrument, which has this shape:

E fay aquela de argent o de eram, dejos streyt, en le qual sia un petit trauc, e am pla desus.
And make it of silver or brass, narrow at the bottom, with a small hole, and flat on top.

E donc sia cum empellent a que es en la conquavitat de la canula de continent aqui on sira necessari.
And then press down on what is in the concavity of the cannula to inject it where necessary.

E si vols, pren le ray e envolveys en la extremitat de lu decentment coto, e aprop gieta oli en la concavitat del auziment, o suc de alcuna herba, o aquo que vols de aquestas medicinas en la canula — e aquela es en la aurelha —, e aprop entromet le ray am coto desus e exprem am la tua ma aquel am compressio temprada de lu entro que l’oli sia expellit en la concavitat del auziment e le malaute senta le oli dedintz.
And if you want, take the rod and wrap the end of it with cotton decently, then pour oil into the concavity of the hearing, or juice of some herb, or whatever you want of these medicines into the cannula — which is in the ear —, then insert the rod with the cotton on it and press it with your hand with tempered compression until the oil is expelled into the hearing concavity and the patient feels the oil inside.

E aquo que sia enfundut en la aurelha sia ja scalfat am foc un petit.
And whatever is poured into the ear should be warmed a little with fire.

E guarda te que la causa la qual distillaras en l’aurelha no sia trop frega, quar las medicinas de aurelha no suffertan aquo.
And beware that the substance you drip into the ear is not too cold, for ear medicines do not tolerate this.

Chapter VII

De curacio de opilacio de aurelhas.
On the treatment of ear blockage.

Alcunas veguadas alcus enfantz yeysso dels ventres de lors mayres no-perforatz, e aquo ja accideys en alcus homes, opilacio en le meat de la aurelha per plagua o per carn nada en aquela; e aquesta opilacio alcunas veguadas es fayta en le preon del trau de la aurelha la qual no consiec le vezement, e alcunas veguadas es en las partidas defora.
Sometimes some children are born from their mothers' wombs with unperforated ears, and this also happens in some men, blockage in the ear canal due to injury or flesh growing in it; and this blockage sometimes occurs in the opening of the ear canal that does not reach the eardrum, and sometimes it is in the outer parts.

E aquela que accideys en le preon de lu, la qual no comprehen le vezement, es en las majors disposicios de dificil sanacio.
And the one that occurs in the opening, which does not affect the eardrum, is in the worst conditions for difficult healing.

E en aquela en qual consegueys le vezement, cove que tu pauses la aurelha del malaute al solelh e reguarda en aquela.
And in the one that affects the eardrum, you should hold the patient's ear to the sun and examine it.

E donc si vezes oppulacio apparent, obre aquela am espatum subtil del qual aquesta es la forma de lu:
And then if you see apparent blockage, open it with a subtle spatula of which this is the shape:

Si’ la extremitat de lu en la qual sia petita la latitut, del qual la una partida sia aguda e l’autra no; e le spatum sia de dos latz leon per so que no fassa lizio al aurelha.
With the end of it having a small width, with one part sharp and the other not; and the spatula should be double-edged so as not to cause injury to the ear.

E donc si la opilacio es per carn nascuda en la aurelha o en le meat de lu, lahoras te aquella am oncis subtils e incidiys aquela vehement facilitat entro que sia ostada tota la carn.
And if the blockage is due to flesh growing in the ear or in the canal, then hold it with subtle forceps and incise it with great care until all the flesh is removed.

E si opilacio es en le preon de la aurelha, lahoras pren ray subtil leon e escalfa aquel am foc un pauc, e aprop met aquel en le meat de l’aurelha.
And if the blockage is in the opening of the ear, then take a fine rod and heat it slightly with fire, and then put it into the ear canal.

E donc si as scentit oppulacio am aquo que le malaute atroba am facilitat, per gravetat de ausir, esforsa la oppressio de lu am facilitat; e guarda te que no plagues les nervis del aurelha.
And then if you have felt the blockage with what the patient finds easy, due to hearing heaviness, ease the pressure on it gently; and take care not to damage the nerves of the ear.

E aprop pausa en l’aurelha drap de li segon la quantitat del ample del trauc lo qual tu as envolvutz en l’enguent egipciac; e fay aquo per alcus jorns entro que sias segur de la consolidacio de la plagua; o que prenguas qualgua e enfon aquela en ayguaros e pulveriza sobre lu segi trissat e usa de lu; e si vezes la aurelha ja esser apostemada, lahoras cove que tu estraguas la qualgua e permutes aquela am autra qualgua la qual tu envoloparas en cerot que sia fayt am oli rozat, entro que sia le apostema caut cedat; e aprop cura aquel entro que sia sanat.
And then place a piece of cloth in the ear according to the width of the opening which you have wrapped in Egyptian ointment; and do this for several days until you are sure of the wound's consolidation; or take wool and soak it in water and sprinkle it over the wound; and if you see the ear already swollen, then you need to remove the wool and replace it with another wool which you will wrap in a plaster made with rose oil, until the swelling has subsided; and then treat it until it is healed.

Mays si accideys flux de sanc, lahoras submergeys esponga o drap en aygua frega e pausa sobre la aurelha, e usa del autra curacio la qual restrengeys le flux del sanc, la qual es dita en le tractat de distillacio.
But if there is a flow of blood, then submerge a sponge or cloth in cold water and place it over the ear, and use another treatment that restricts the blood flow, which is mentioned in the treatise on distillation.

De cura de athezil le qual endeveno a las palpebras.
On the treatment of styes that occur on the eyelids.

Athezil les quals endeveno a las paupebras dels huels alcuna veguada so humitz, alscunas veguadas son secs.
Styes that occur on the eyelids of the eyes are sometimes wet, sometimes dry.

Donc cove que tu tenguas aquels am tenhalhas o am onci, e inscindas aquels am espatum de las suas razitz, e aprop pausa sobre les locs segi trissat.
Then you need to hold them with forceps or pincers, and cut them with a spatula from their roots, and then place crushed herbs over the spots.

But if pain with a flow of blood comes, then you cauterize them with a fine lancet cautery.

E la milhor cauterizacio es aprop las inscizios e tropas veguadas retornam quan reman alcuna causa de sas razitz; e quan so cauterizadas am foc, aquelas razitz son comburidas e no retornan.
And the best cauterization is after the incisions and sometimes returning when some root cause remains; and when they are cauterized with fire, those roots are burned and do not return.
