Chapter fifteen on cauterizing the relaxation of the eyelid.
If the eyelid becomes flaccid due to illness or moisture, then cauterize the eyelid once with this crescent-shaped cautery iron.
And if you wish, cauterize him slightly above the eyebrows, two cauterizations on each side, and keep away from the temples.
And the length of each cauterization should be the length of the eyebrow.
And do not overdo your hand with the cautery, but only to the extent that a third of the skin is burned.
And the shape of the cautery iron should be according to this description.
