Chapter sixty-eight on the relaxation of the scrotum's skin.
Often the skin of the testicles relaxes in some people without the flesh inside them relaxing, and their appearance becomes ugly.
So it is appropriate for whoever wishes to treat it to put the patient on his back and cut all the skin that has relaxed on the thick skin, then bring the two edges together.
And if you wish, first sew the excess relaxed skin with three or four stitches, and make sure of the stitching, then cut between the stitches, then treat it with the treatment of other wounds until it heals and the threads fall out.
