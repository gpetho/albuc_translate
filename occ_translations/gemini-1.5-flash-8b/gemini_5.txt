E si es foro-getada am gesti, bo es; e sino, lahoras aministra la extraccio de lu am onci subtil de pauca duplicacio.	And if it is expelled with the instrument, good.  Otherwise, administer its extraction with a subtle, thin, small spoon.

And if it is still not expelled by that, then make a brass cannula and insert the end of the cannula into the hole in the ear, and seal around the cannula with softened wax and oil so that only the cannula is open.

But if it is not expelled in this manner, then take a heated cauldrons or a suitable device with which birds are caught, a small amount, and place it on the end of the wound, and then wrap it properly over that placed cloth.
E aprop entromet aquel en le trauc de la aurelha am facilitat, e aprop excicca la aurelha de la humiditat.	And then insert that into the ear's hole with ease, and then dry the ear of moisture.

And therefore, if it is not expelled by all the methods we have described, then you should consider cutting it out before it becomes a painful abscess or spasm.

And the way to cut it is that you first bleed the patient from the cephalic vein and extract blood according to its virtue, and then make that incision between your hands and turn the ear towards the upper parts, and make a small incision at the base of the ear towards its fat, in the location firmly pressed by you.
E sia la fissura de figura lunar entro que pervengua a la peyra, e, la peyra atrobada, osta aquela si podes am instrumentz apropriatz; e aprop cos la fissura en aquela hora am velocitat, e cura aquela entro que sia sanat.	Let the incision be crescent-shaped until it reaches the stone, and once the stone is found, remove it if you can with appropriate instruments; then close the incision at that time quickly, and treat it until it is healed.
E si la causa cazent en la aurelha es un dels gras que creysso e so inflatz, lahoras aministra la curacio e la extraccio de lu; mays si no obezeys a tu ad eyssiment, lahoras tu pren spatum teune subtil segon aquesta forma:	And if the cause of the affliction in the ear is one of the fats that grows and swells, then administer its cure and extraction; but if you do not obey this prescription, then you should take a subtle spatum in this way:
e aministra am aquel la abscizio de aquel gra lo qual es cazut en l’aurelha.	And administer with that the excision of that fat which has fallen into the ear.

And do not do this when you think that it has already been softened by the ear's moisture, and break it into small pieces, and then extract them with tweezers or by skillful manipulation, or with an incision as we have said, for this will ease its extraction.

The patient should insert one into the ear canal, and place a foot on that side, and strike the stone with another stone on the outside of the ear.
Donc no cesse aquo am peyra entro que tota l’aygua sia foro-getada.	So do not stop with the stone until all the water is expelled.

And when the water is expelled in this way, take a piece of paper or linen, put one end into the ear, and hold the other end in a fire until most of it is burned.
E prop met i autre gonc e fay aquo tantas veguadas entro que tota l’aygua sia foro-getada, e stratz aquel am canula ayssi co desus es dit de la peyra extrasadora.	And put in more linen, and do this many times until all the water is expelled, and extract it with a tube, as described above concerning the stone used for extraction.

However, if a small creature, such as an insect or similar, has entered the ear, treat it as described in the section on the various kinds of ear maladies; but if it is a large creature causing an obvious obstruction, then remove it with forceps and hooks, and its removal is easier than any other thing that might be lodged in the ear.
Mays en la extraccio de verm engendrat en la aurelha, quan tu curas aquel am aquo que havem dit en la divisio e en le tractat de distillacios e no comfereys la tua curacio, lahoras cove que tu reguardes la aurelha al solelh.	But in the removal of a worm that has grown in the ear, when you treat it with what we have said in the section on ear problems and in the treatise on distillations, and your treatment does not work, then you should expose the ear to sunlight.
E donc si apar aqui alcuna causa dels verms, lahoras extratz aquel am gesti o am onci subtils, mays si no apar deguna causa, lahoras pren una canula dejos streyta e desus ampla de la qual aquesta es la forma de lu:	And if any worm-like object is visible, then remove it with delicate forceps or hooks; but if no object is visible, then take a narrow-mouthed, wide-bellied tube, of which this is the shape:
E entromet la extremitat subtil en la aurelha segon la quantitat la qual suffertar puesca le malaute; e aprop sucgeys am aquela per succio fort, e fay aquo motas veguadas, entro que totz les verms sian foro-gitatz.	And insert the narrow end into the ear according to the amount the patient can tolerate; then, forcefully suck with it, repeating this many times, until all the worms have been removed.
Mays si no obezeysso a tu al ayssiment, lahoras tu opila e claus en le circuit de la canula am cera ayssi cum havem dit; lahoras aministre distillacios las quals han ja esproadas les antics en la curacio dels verms, e atrobaras aquo en le tractat de distillacios.	But if it does not yield to this treatment, then seal the area around the tube with wax, as we have described; then administer distillations, which the ancients have already proven effective in treating worms, and you will find this in the treatise on distillations.

E fay aquela de argent o de eram, dejos streyt, en le qual sia un petit trauc, e am pla desus.	Make it of silver or brass, narrow-ended, with a small hole and a flat top.
E donc sia cum empellent a que es en la conquavitat de la canula de continent aqui on sira necessari.	And thus it should push against whatever is in the cavity of the tube, continually, wherever necessary.
E si vols, pren le ray e envolveys en la extremitat de lu decentment coto, e aprop gieta oli en la concavitat del auziment, o suc de alcuna herba, o aquo que vols de aquestas medicinas en la canula — e aquela es en la aurelha —, e aprop entromet le ray am coto desus e exprem am la tua ma aquel am compressio temprada de lu entro que l’oli sia expellit en la concavitat del auziment e le malaute senta le oli dedintz.	And if you wish, take the reed and wrap its end properly with wool, then pour oil into the concavity of the instrument, or the juice of any herb, or whatever medicine you want into the tube—and that is in the ear—and then insert the reed with wool on top, and press it with your hand gently until the oil is expelled into the cavity of the instrument, and the patient feels the oil inside.
E aquo que sia enfundut en la aurelha sia ja scalfat am foc un petit.	And whatever is put into the ear should be heated a little with fire.

De curacio de opilacio de aurelhas.	On the treatment of ear obstructions.
Alcunas veguadas alcus enfantz yeysso dels ventres de lors mayres no-perforatz, e aquo ja accideys en alcus homes, opilacio en le meat de la aurelha per plagua o per carn nada en aquela; e aquesta opilacio alcunas veguadas es fayta en le preon del trau de la aurelha la qual no consiec le vezement, e alcunas veguadas es en las partidas defora.	Sometimes children inherit this from their mothers' wombs, and it happens in some men too; obstruction in the ear canal due to a plague or flesh growing there; and this obstruction is sometimes in the membrane of the ear canal which does not allow hearing, and sometimes it is in the outer parts.

Cura de grandeyssa que veno a las palpebras dels huels.	Treatment of swelling that comes to the eyelids of the eyes.

De la cura axcirnah en la palpebra desus del huel.	Of the treatment of a boil on the eyelid above the eye.
Axcirnath es grays le qual es engendrat en las tunicas de la palpebra desus, e majorment endeve als enfantz, agrava les huels de aquels et accideyso a lor catartz le mayti; e no podon reguardar la claror del solelh per aquo quar lagremas se coytan ad aquels. Capitol .xi.	Chapter 11.
De manieyras de tactimi del huel.	Methods of treating the eye.
Quan en las palpebras dels huels so natz pels ajustatz dejos les pels naturals e so antiquatz, lahoras aquels noso al huel e fan endevenir manieras de malautias ayssi cum lacrimas assiduas e mollificacio de palpebras e tela entro que aquo sia causa de destruccio del huel que s’apela tactinir del huel, e cura se en quatre manieyras: o per cauteri am foc; o am medicina acuta segon que denant es dit en le capitol de cauteri; o es fayt atactini per inscizio e sutura; o per cannas segon que yeu diyre.	When in the eyelids of the eyes there are growths underneath the natural skin and they become inflamed, this affects the eye and causes various ailments such as constant tears, softening of the eyelids and tissue, eventually leading to the destruction of the eye, which is called an eye ailment (or tactus of the eye).  It is treated in four ways: either by cautery with fire; or with a sharp medicine, as previously described in the chapter on cautery; or it is treated with incisions and sutures; or with probes, as I will explain.
E donc cove que tu pauses le cap del malaute en le tieu se; aprop converteys la palpebra del uelh am la tua ma sinistra, e si es convertida, bo es; e sino, entromet agulha en aquela en la qual sia fiel de la partida pus dejos de la palpebra, e fay penetrar la agulha am fiel desus, e aquo sia — prop del marge de la palpebra en le qual naysso les pels — prop dels pels, e tira le fiel a las partidas desus am la palpebra, e converteys aquela am le ray.	And therefore you should place the patient's head on your lap; then gently hold the eyelid with your left hand, and if it is turned outwards, it is good; and if not, insert a needle into the part where the flesh is closest to the eyelid underneath,  and push the needle through with a thread above, right beside the edge of the eyelid where the eyelashes grow — close to the eyelashes —  and pull the thread outward with the eyelid, and turn the eyelid with the tool.
E aprop fen en la partida dedintz de la palpebra dejos les pels ajustatz am spatum inscidentz del lacrimal major entro al lacrimal mendre; e aprop extratz le fiel e pausa dejos la palpebra polvilhs petitz de coto o de drap, e aprop senha sobre la palpebra am tenta, e sia ayssi cum figura de fuelha de mirt.	Then, using a sharp instrument (spatum) cut through the inflamed area within the eyelid, underneath the skin, from the larger lacrimal duct to the smaller one; and then remove the thread and place small cotton or cloth powders under the eyelid. Then, bandage the eyelid in the shape of a myrtle leaf.
Mays empero cove que sia la figura segon la quantitat de helevacio de la palpebra, quar aquo ja es diversificat en les homes, quar de aquels es le quals indigeys que sia inscidit de la palpebra gran quantitat segon la quantitat per la qual la palpebra es mollificada.	However, the bandage shape should correspond to the extent of the eyelid's swelling, as this varies from person to person. Some individuals require a larger portion of the eyelid to be incised depending on the degree of softening.
E aprop fen am le spatum sobre las doas linhas las quals has senhadas, e comensa del lacrimal major entro al lacrimal menor.	Then, using the spatula, make incisions along the two lines you have marked, starting from the larger lacrimal duct and extending to the smaller one.
E si’ la tua fissura prop dels pels naturals ayssi cum es la grossetut del rey.	And if the incision is close to the natural eyelashes, just as wide as the king's finger.
E aprop met le onci en un dels dos angles de la codena, e aprop excoria aquela tota, e aprop ajusta am la scitura las doas labias am gula o fiel de lana subtil, e abstergeys le sanc, e fay adherir aquo que superflueys dels fiels sobre dos sobrecils am alcunas causas enviscans, si voles far aquo, e sino, non cures.	Then, place a ligature at one of the two corners of the wound, and then scrape the entire area.  Then, using thread, or fine wool, bring the two lips of the wound together.  Clean away the blood.  If you wish, use other substances to help the extra threads adhere to the two eyebrows; otherwise, don't worry about it.
E aprop leyssa la sutura dels fiels entro a tres dias o quatre, e aprop cura aquo.	And leave the stitches of thread in place for three or four days, then treat the wound.
E si vols, layssa la plagua ses sutura, e cura aquela am aquo que exsicca e constrenh, quar la palpebra es alevada deves la sigillacio de la plagua e la agreguacio de lu; mays sutura es milor E donc aysso diysso les antics de taxanir.
