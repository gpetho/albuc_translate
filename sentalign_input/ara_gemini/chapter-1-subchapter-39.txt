Chapter thirty-nine on cauterizing the uterus.
If a substance of coldness and humidities occurs in the uterus, it thereby prevents pregnancy.
And its menses are corrupted and its flow is difficult.
Or pain occurs to her at its arrival.
Then it is appropriate to cauterize three cauterizations around the navel as we mentioned in the cauterization of the bladder.
And a cauterization on the coccyx at the bottom of the back or two cauterizations.
And the cautery iron should be nail-shaped.
