Chapter nine on cauterizing paralysis and the relaxation of the entire body.
It is necessary to proceed in purifying the head with the irriagations and what we have mentioned.
Then shave the head of the sick person.
Then cauterize him with a cautery in the middle of the head and a cautery on each horn of the head and a cautery on its back and three on the vertebrae of the neck.
If you need more than that in the illness of relaxation of the body and the patient can tolerate it and the disease is strong and entrenched.
Then cauterize him with four cauteries on the vertebrae of his back.
And intensify the cautery until you burn most of the skin.
And lift your hand.
Then treat him according to what has been mentioned before until he recovers, God willing.
And the cautery iron should be olive-shaped.
