Chapter Forty-Two:
On the cauterization for back pain.
Sometimes pain is in the back due to various causes, such as a fall, an impact, excessive evacuation, and similar reasons, due to the infusion of cold and humid matter.
And cauterization is only applicable in this specific type caused by the infusion of cold matter.
Therefore, it is necessary, after evacuating the patient with purging pills or similar, to make three lines on the painful back area according to its width, marking with ink five or more cauteries on each line, depending on the patient's tolerance and the strength of the cautery.
The cauterization should be done with a pointed cautery.
