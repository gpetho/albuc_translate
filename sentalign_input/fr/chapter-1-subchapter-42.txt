When pain comes to the back, either from falling or impact or excessive evacuation, or from the spread of cold matter, cautery is useful for none of these cases, except for one caused by the infusion of cold matter.
It is necessary that after the patient is purged with fetid pills or similar, three lines be made on his back where the pain is, marked with ink, and in each line five cauteries or more according to the strength and capacity of the patient; and let the cautery be done with the cautery called "poins"; and if you wish, make three or four cauteries with the medium clavile cautery in this shape:
