About tartar
Sometimes, inside the mouth between the gums, tartar that is unsightly and rough forms on the teeth; and sometimes the teeth become black, yellow, and green, to the point that the corruption reaches the gums, and the teeth become foul-smelling; then you must make the patient sit between your hands, with his head in your lap, and scrape the teeth where the tartar appears until nothing remains; and do the same for the blackness, greenness, yellowness, and other stains, until they are cleaned; and if you remove what is there at the first scraping, that is good; if not, then restart the next day and the third day, until you have scraped everything; and know that teeth require various forms of instruments for scraping, according to what the procedure shows, because the scrapers used for the outer teeth have different forms than those used for the inner teeth; and have these forms and others prepared with you.
