When you treat it according to what we have said in the division, and it does not help, then give the patient three nights of cochies (a sort of medicinal drink) to drink;
afterwards shave the head and apply a cautery in the middle of the olive-shaped cautery mark;
and make two cauteries with the cautery called "clavilis" above the two eyebrows, slightly below the hairline;
and beware of the artery so that you do not cut it.
