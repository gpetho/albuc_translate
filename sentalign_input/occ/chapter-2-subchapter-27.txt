Chapter XXVII.
On the extraction of those causes that come in the two lips in the inner parts.
Many times it happens to many men that in the inner parts of their lips there are hard abscesses resembling grains of barley, and some of them are smaller and some are larger.
So you must turn the lips and cut each of those hardnesses from every part, and then fill the site of the excision with pounded sage until the blood is stopped, and then wash it with sour wine and treat the site with what is astringent, until it is healed.
