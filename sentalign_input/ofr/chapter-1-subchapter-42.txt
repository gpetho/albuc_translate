of the pain of the back
When pain comes to the back, either by falling or from a blow or by too great an evacuation, or from some sort of spread cold matter, for none of these cases is cautery useful, except for the one that comes from the infusion of cold matter.
It is necessary after the patient has been purged with foul-smelling pills or other such, that three lines marked with ink be made on his back, there where the pain is located, and on each line five cauteries or more according to the strength and the amount that the patient can bear;
and the cautery should be done according to the cautery which is called "points";
