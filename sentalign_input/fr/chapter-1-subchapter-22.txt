When scrofula is caused by phlegm and cold moisture, and does not respond to digestion by medicines, then heat the cautery whose shape will be mentioned later, and it should have two openings for the fumes to escape on the other side; and apply the hot cautery on the abscess once, or twice if needed, until it reaches the bottom of the abscess; and if the abscess is small, apply the cautery according to the size of the abscess, and leave it for three days; then apply cotton soaked in butter until the burn is gone; afterward treat the area with ointments and bandages until healed.
